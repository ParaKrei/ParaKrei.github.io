This is my first publically released mod for any game, and it's one I'm quite happy with!

This mod is for the fan-made game Sonic Robo Blast 2, and was inspired by the Sonic the Hedgehog 3 ROM hack Hellfire Saga.

My mod replicates the health system from Hellfire Saga, giving players the ability to give themselves a challenge.

The mod is designed to be heavily customizable, giving users the ability to tweak the mod to their liking.
